Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 28, 2026

SnapBudget ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection

SnapBudget is designed as a local-first application. We collect:

  • Financial Data: Expense amounts, merchant names, and categories.
  • Images and Text: Images of receipts and extracted text processed via OCR.

2. Camera and Device Permissions

To provide core features, the App requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: Required to take photos of receipts for automated scanning.
  • Storage: Required to upload receipt images from your device gallery.

3. Use of Data and AI Processing

We use your data to provide expense tracking services. We utilize Google Gemini AI and Google ML Kit to process receipt information. Text extracted from your receipts is sent to these services for analysis and categorization to improve accuracy.

4. Data Retention

Your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal financial records on our servers. Deleting the App or clearing App data will remove this information.

5. Security

We prioritize the security of your data. However, as the data resides on your device, we recommend using device-level security (passcodes, biometrics) to protect your information.
