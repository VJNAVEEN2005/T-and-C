Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 01, 2026

Expense Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application.

Core Privacy Principle: Your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any central servers that store your personal transaction history.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our AI-powered expense tracking features, the app requires access to:

  • Camera: Used exclusively for scanning physical receipts and payment screenshots.
  • Photo Library: Used only when you manually select an image of a receipt to be analyzed.
  • AI Analysis Data: When you scan a receipt, the image or text is sent to Google Gemini AI models for data extraction (Amount, Merchant, and Category).

2. Data Processing & Storage

  • Local Storage: All transaction records, categories, and settings are stored locally on your device using encrypted local databases (Hive).
  • Third-Party AI: We use Google Gemini AI services. By using the AI scanning features, you acknowledge that receipt data is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy standards.
  • No Cloud Sync: This version of the app does not sync data to any cloud account. If you delete the app or lose your device, your data cannot be recovered by us.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard local security measures. However, the security of your data also depends on your device security (Passcode, Biometrics). We recommend keeping your device updated and secured.
